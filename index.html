Chantelle McColl

Chantelle McColl is a writer based in Melbourne, Narrm, who specialises in video games and analysing
how they convey memorable narratives and experiences.
Dabbling in indie genres like platformers, Metroidvanias and all games story-rich,
Chantelle is always on the lookout for the next experimental game.
You can find her work on Checkpoint Gaming, Byteside, ScreenHub Australia and on Twitter.
